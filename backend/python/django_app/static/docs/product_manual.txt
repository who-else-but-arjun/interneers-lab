PRODUCT MANUAL - Inventory Management System
==========================================

Version: 2.0
Last Updated: March 2026

SECTION 1: OVERVIEW
-------------------
The Inventory Management System is designed to help businesses track, manage, and optimize their product inventory. This system supports multiple product categories, brands, and provides AI-powered assistance for inventory operations.

SECTION 2: PRODUCT SPECIFICATIONS
-----------------------------------
Each product in the system contains the following fields:
- Product Name: Unique identifier for the product
- Brand: Manufacturer or supplier brand
- Category: Classification group (Electronics, Furniture, Clothing, etc.)
- Description: Detailed product information
- Price: Unit price in INR (Indian Rupees)
- Quantity: Current stock level
- Policy: Product-specific policies (warranty, returns, etc.)

SECTION 3: WARRANTY INFORMATION
---------------------------------
Standard Warranty Terms:
- Electronics: 1-year manufacturer warranty
- Furniture: 2-year structural warranty
- Clothing/Apparel: 30-day defect warranty
- Appliances: 2-year comprehensive warranty
- Books: No warranty, exchange only for damaged items
- Toys: 6-month safety warranty

Extended Warranty:
Customers can purchase extended warranty for an additional 20% of product price.
Extended warranty adds 1-2 years depending on product category.

SECTION 4: PRODUCT CATEGORIES
-------------------------------
Supported Categories:
1. Electronics - Smartphones, laptops, tablets, accessories
2. Furniture - Office chairs, desks, storage units
3. Clothing - Men's, Women's, Children's apparel
4. Home Appliances - Kitchen appliances, cleaning equipment
5. Books - Fiction, Non-fiction, Educational
6. Sports & Fitness - Equipment, apparel, accessories
7. Toys & Games - Educational toys, board games, outdoor toys
8. Beauty & Personal Care - Cosmetics, skincare, grooming
9. Automotive - Car accessories, maintenance tools
10. Office Supplies - Stationery, printers, consumables

SECTION 5: INVENTORY OPERATIONS
-------------------------------
Adding Products:
- Single product entry through dashboard
- Bulk upload via CSV file
- AI-assisted product generation

Updating Stock:
- Manual quantity adjustment
- Automatic low-stock alerts (threshold: 5 units)
- Stock level tracking with historical data

Removing Products:
- Soft delete with audit trail
- Bulk deletion capabilities
- Category-wise removal options

SECTION 6: AI INTEGRATION
-------------------------
The system includes AI-powered features:
- Semantic search for product discovery
- Similar product recommendations
- Natural language product generation
- Smart inventory insights and analytics

SECTION 7: DATA EXPORT/IMPORT
-----------------------------
Supported Formats:
- CSV for bulk operations
- JSON for API integrations
- Excel for reporting (via CSV conversion)

SECTION 8: SECURITY & ACCESS
----------------------------
- CSRF protection on all forms
- API authentication for external access
- Data validation on all inputs
- Audit logging for inventory changes
