VENDOR FAQs - Inventory Management System
=========================================

Document Version: 2.5
Last Updated: March 2026

===============================================================================
SECTION 1: GETTING STARTED
===============================================================================

Q1: How do I become a registered vendor?
A: To become a vendor:
   1. Complete the online vendor registration form
   2. Submit business documents (GST, PAN, Bank details)
   3. Complete product catalog verification
   4. Sign vendor agreement
   5. Integration testing with our API
   6. Go live approval (typically 7-10 business days)

Q2: What are the vendor eligibility criteria?
A: Requirements include:
   - Valid GST registration
   - Minimum 1 year in business
   - Product quality certifications (where applicable)
   - Ability to fulfill orders within 24-48 hours
   - Acceptable return/refund policy alignment
   - INR 50,000 minimum monthly commitment (for preferred vendors)

Q3: Is there a registration fee?
A: Standard registration is FREE. Premium vendor status with featured 
   placement has a monthly fee of INR 5,000.

Q4: How long does the approval process take?
A: Standard approval: 5-7 business days
   Premium/fast-track: 2-3 business days (with additional fee)
   Re-verification: Required annually

===============================================================================
SECTION 2: PRODUCT LISTING
===============================================================================

Q5: How do I add products to the system?
A: Three methods available:
   - Single product entry via vendor dashboard
   - Bulk CSV upload (up to 1000 products at once)
   - API integration for automated sync
   - AI-assisted product generation (beta feature)

Q6: What product information is required?
A: Mandatory fields:
   - Product name (max 100 characters)
   - Brand name
   - Category (from approved list)
   - Price (INR)
   - Available quantity
   - Product description (min 50 characters)
   - At least 3 product images
   - Product weight and dimensions
   - Warranty/return policy details

Q7: Can I list products in multiple categories?
A: No, each product must have one primary category. However, products 
   appear in search results for related category searches through our 
   AI-powered semantic search.

Q8: How do I set competitive pricing?
A: The system provides:
   - Market price analysis tool
   - Competitor price tracking
   - Dynamic pricing suggestions
   - Seasonal pricing recommendations
   Note: Minimum 10% margin required for system sustainability

Q9: Are there restricted products?
A: Prohibited items include:
   - Counterfeit or replica goods
   - Hazardous materials without proper certification
   - Prescription medications
   - Weapons and ammunition
   - Adult content/products
   - Items violating intellectual property rights

Q10: Can I update product information after listing?
A: Yes, updates possible for:
    - Price changes (effective immediately or scheduled)
    - Quantity/stock updates (real-time)
    - Description updates
    - Image updates
    - Policy changes (requires approval)
    Note: Name and category changes require manual review

===============================================================================
SECTION 3: ORDER MANAGEMENT
===============================================================================

Q11: How will I receive orders?
A: Order notifications via:
    - Real-time dashboard alerts
    - Email notifications
    - SMS alerts (optional)
    - API webhooks for integrated systems
    - Mobile app push notifications

Q12: What is the order fulfillment timeframe?
A: Standard: Confirm within 4 hours, ship within 24 hours
   Prime: Confirm within 2 hours, ship within 12 hours
   Same-day: Confirm within 1 hour, deliver same day (select cities)

Q13: How do I handle out-of-stock situations?
A: Options:
    - Automatic stock sync prevents overselling
    - Set "low stock" alerts (default: 5 units)
    - Enable "backorder" for specific products
    - Temporary deactivation of product listing
    - Partial fulfillment option for multi-item orders

Q14: Can I cancel an order?
A: Vendor cancellation allowed within 2 hours of order placement if:
    - Item genuinely unavailable
    - Pricing error discovered
    - Force majeure situations
    Note: Excessive cancellations affect vendor rating

Q15: How are shipping costs calculated?
A: Shipping calculated based on:
    - Product weight and dimensions
    - Delivery location
    - Shipping method chosen by customer
    - Vendor can offer free shipping above threshold
    - Integration with multiple courier partners for best rates

SECTION 4: PAYMENTS & FINANCE
===============================================================================

Q16: What is the payment cycle?
A: Standard payment terms:
    - Net 15 days from delivery confirmation
    - Weekly payments for high-volume vendors (eligibility criteria)
    - Payment processed every Tuesday and Friday
    - Minimum payout threshold: INR 1,000

Q17: What are the commission rates?
A: Category-wise commission:
    - Electronics: 5-8%
    - Fashion: 12-15%
    - Home & Living: 8-10%
    - Beauty: 10-12%
    - Books: 8-10%
    - Toys: 10-12%
    - Sports: 8-10%
    - Premium brands: Negotiated separately

Q18: Are there any additional fees?
A: Additional charges:
    - Payment gateway fee: 2% per transaction
    - Storage fee (FBA): INR 15/cubic foot/month
    - Returns processing: INR 50 per return
    - Advertising fees: Optional, pay-per-click

Q19: How do I track my payments?
A: Available through:
    - Vendor dashboard payment section
    - Detailed transaction reports
    - Invoice download
    - Payment reconciliation tool
    - GST-compliant invoice generation

Q20: What about GST handling?
A: System handles:
    - Automatic GST calculation (IGST/CGST/SGST)
    - Monthly GST reports
    - TDS deduction where applicable
    - Integration with GST portal for filing assistance

===============================================================================
SECTION 5: RETURNS & REFUNDS
===============================================================================

Q21: Who handles returns?
A: Return process:
    - Customer initiates return request
    - Vendor reviews within 24 hours
    - Approved returns: Pickup arranged by platform
    - Item inspected at fulfillment center
    - Refund processed after quality check

Q22: What if a customer returns a damaged product?
A: Scenarios handled:
    - Customer damage: No refund, item returned to customer
    - Transit damage: Courier liability, vendor protected
    - Defective product: Full refund, vendor bear cost
    - Wrong item sent: Full refund + penalty, vendor cost

Q23: How are return shipping charges handled?
A: Return shipping:
    - Customer change of mind: Customer pays
    - Defective/wrong item: Platform pays
    - Size/fit issues: Customer pays (fashion category)
    - Damaged in transit: Courier pays

Q24: Can I dispute a return?
A: Dispute process:
    - Lodge dispute within 48 hours of return initiation
    - Provide evidence (photos, videos, documentation)
    - Resolution within 5 business days
    - Appeal option available

SECTION 6: PERFORMANCE & METRICS
===============================================================================

Q25: How is vendor performance measured?
A: Key metrics tracked:
    - Order fulfillment rate (target: >95%)
    - On-time shipping (target: >98%)
    - Cancellation rate (target: <2%)
    - Return rate (target: <5%)
    - Customer rating (target: >4.0/5)
    - Response time (target: <4 hours)

Q26: What happens if performance drops?
A: Performance tiers:
    - Green (excellent): Featured placement, lower commission
    - Yellow (good): Standard treatment
    - Orange (needs improvement): Warning, training provided
    - Red (poor): Listing limitations, increased commission
    - Black (critical): Account suspension review

Q27: How can I improve my visibility?
A: Optimization strategies:
    - Complete product information
    - High-quality images (min 1000x1000px)
    - Competitive pricing
    - Fast shipping options
    - Positive customer reviews
    - Participate in promotional campaigns
    - Use sponsored product ads

Q28: Are there vendor rewards programs?
A: Yes, benefits include:
    - Sales milestone bonuses
    - Reduced commission for consistent performance
    - Early access to new features
    - Dedicated account manager (top vendors)
    - Marketing co-op funds
    - Annual vendor conference invitation

SECTION 7: TECHNICAL INTEGRATION
===============================================================================

Q29: Do you provide API access?
A: Yes, comprehensive REST API available for:
    - Product catalog management
    - Order processing
    - Inventory synchronization
    - Shipping updates
    - Returns management
    - Reports and analytics
    Rate limit: 1000 requests/hour (standard), 5000 (premium)

Q30: What is the API authentication method?
A: Security:
    - OAuth 2.0 authentication
    - API key + Secret combination
    - IP whitelisting option
    - Webhook signature verification
    - Rate limiting per vendor account

Q31: Do you offer integration support?
A: Support levels:
    - Documentation and code samples (free)
    - Email support for integration queries
    - Phone support for premium vendors
    - Dedicated integration consultant (enterprise)
    - Pre-built plugins for major e-commerce platforms

Q32: How does inventory sync work?
A: Synchronization options:
    - Real-time API updates (recommended)
    - Scheduled CSV uploads
    - Automatic stock reservation during checkout
    - Low stock alerts via webhook
    - Buffer stock settings to prevent overselling

SECTION 8: POLICIES & COMPLIANCE
===============================================================================

Q33: What are the product quality requirements?
A: Quality standards:
    - Genuine products only (no counterfeits)
    - Compliance with Indian standards (BIS where applicable)
    - Proper labeling and packaging
    - Expiry date management for applicable products
    - Safety certifications for electronic products

Q34: How is customer data protected?
A: Privacy compliance:
    - Vendor access limited to order-fulfillment data only
    - No access to customer contact information
    - Communication through platform messaging only
    - GDPR and PDP Bill compliance
    - Regular security audits

Q35: What about intellectual property?
A: IP protection:
    - Vendors warrant they have rights to sell listed products
    - Trademark verification for brand-name products
    - DMCA takedown process for reported violations
    - Counterfeit detection program
    - Legal action for repeat offenders

Q36: Are there advertising guidelines?
A: Marketing rules:
    - Truthful product representations
    - No fake reviews or ratings manipulation
    - Compliance with ASCI guidelines
    - Proper disclosure of sponsored content
    - No misleading discount claims
